How to use

  • create a room by either using the bookmarklet from a youtube-video
  • or by logging in and creating a room by hand via the youtube video-id.
  • send the link to your room to other people so they can join you.
  • Each user will see the video and participants. If you start/stop the video, it will be started/stopped for all other participants
  • Bullets (shoutouts) can be send by every user and will be shown as an overlay over the video together with a picture of the user.
